Rome — The Eternal City

Rome, capital of Italy, is celebrated worldwide as the “Eternal City.” Founded, according to legend, by Romulus in 753 BC, Rome grew into the heart of an empire whose art, law, and engineering shaped the Western world. Today Rome blends ancient ruins, Renaissance masterpieces, and lively modern neighborhoods.

Things to See in Rome

  • The Colosseum
  • The Pantheon
  • The Trevi Fountain
  • Piazza Navona
  • St. Peter’s Basilica
